9 Central Park


Welcome to 9 Central Park by Megaworld offers a tranquil uptown lifestyle in one of New York’s most prestigious neighborhoods near the Hudson River. The residences feature expansive windows and top-tier amenities that provide stunning views of the city. Additionally, it offers a vibrant downtown lifestyle, being close to Tribeca, Soho, and West Village. These areas are known for their luxurious commercial districts, filled with boutique shops and fine dining restaurants. The development is designed to cater to those who appreciate both serenity and the bustling city life. Residents can enjoy the best of both worlds, with easy access to peaceful parks and lively urban centers. The architectural design is inspired by New York’s iconic buildings, blending modernity with classic elegance. Each unit is equipped with smart-home technology, ensuring convenience and security for its residents. The community also boasts a range of lifestyle amenities, including a fitness center, swimming pool, and co-working spaces. The development’s strategic location offers unparalleled convenience for both work and leisure. Residents can indulge in the finest shopping and dining experiences just a short walk away. The serene environment near the Hudson River provides a perfect escape from the city’s hustle and bustle. The combination of uptown tranquility and downtown vibrancy makes 9 Central Park an ideal place to live.

Northwin Global City


Welcome to Northwin Global City, a new destination that’s set to transform Bulacan into a thriving center for business and leisure. Just 20 kilometers north of Metro Manila along the North Luzon Expressway (NLEX), this development is all about redefining the area’s economic and cultural landscape. Northwin Global City will feature themed commercial areas, high-rise condominiums, luxury hotels, and lively malls, offering everything you need for modern living. Sustainability is a key focus, with eco-friendly infrastructure and practices designed to benefit both the community and the environment. From solar-powered streetlights and underground cabling to bike lanes, we’re committed to reducing our environmental impact. The development also includes flood prevention measures and an intermodal transport terminal for easy travel. Conveniently located in Marilao and Bocaue, Northwin Global City is just minutes from the Marilao Exit of NLEX, making it easily accessible. We’re also close to the planned New Manila International Airport in Bulacan and the Philippine Arena, further enhancing our connectivity. The development is designed for people who want both peace and the energy of city life. You’ll have access to tranquil parks as well as vibrant urban spaces. The architecture blends modern design with classic elegance, and each unit comes equipped with smart-home technology for convenience and security. Residents can also enjoy amenities like a fitness center, swimming pool, and co-working spaces, making Northwin Global City the perfect place to live, work, and relax.
