Park Mckinley West


Welcome to Park McKinley West, Megaworld’s newest residential project, is situated in the prestigious McKinley West township in Fort Bonifacio, Taguig City. This luxurious condominium complex embodies modern European elegance and offers a variety of unit options, ranging from one to five bedrooms. Residents can indulge in a host of amenities, including a swimming pool, outdoor yoga deck, game room, and sky garden. Its strategic location ensures easy access to major business districts and Ninoy Aquino International Airport Terminal 3. Designed by the renowned Broadway Malyan, Park McKinley West also includes a mall and retail spaces at the podium levels, providing unparalleled convenience and a vibrant lifestyle. The development’s modern design and high-end features make it an ideal choice for those seeking a sophisticated urban living experience. With 24-hour security and CCTV in common areas, residents can enjoy peace of mind. The project is also close to international schools, malls, and multinational offices, enhancing its appeal to families and professionals alike. Park McKinley West truly offers a blend of luxury, convenience, and accessibility, making it a standout residential option in Metro Manila.
