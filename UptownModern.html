Uptown Modern


In today’s rapidly changing world, adapting to the new normal is essential, requiring environments that not only anticipate but also embrace change. Uptown Modern, the latest high-rise residential development in Uptown Bonifacio, sets a new benchmark for contemporary living. Developed by Megaworld, it is part of the largest township in Fort Bonifacio, which includes the iconic Venice Grand Canal Mall, a key feature of Metro Manila’s urban landscape. Located near prestigious areas like Forbes Park and Dasmarinas Village, Uptown Modern introduces the township concept to Fort Bonifacio. Drawing inspiration from leading global thinkers, Uptown Modern is designed to represent the future of residential developments. It emphasizes the comfort, convenience, and security of its residents. The development features state-of-the-art amenities and sustainable design elements. It offers a variety of unit types to cater to different lifestyles. Uptown Modern is not just a place to live but a community that fosters a connected and seamless lifestyle.
